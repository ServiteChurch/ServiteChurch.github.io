Mass Times

Normal masses have resumed. Please note that times are slightly different to those prior to the pandemic. This information is current as of 29th July 2022.

Social distancing guidelines have been relaxed. However, as Covid is still prevalent, we strongly encourage people to wear masks in Church at all times and to only socialise outside of the church and not in the colonnade.

Please note the Church will not be open outside of mass times and the times of special events listed below. See the special events section below for information about private prayer and confessions.

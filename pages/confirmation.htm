2022 Confirmation Programme

Parishioners aged 14 and over are eligible to take part in our Parish Confirmation Programme for 2022. You need to have registered in December 2021. If you havn't yet registered please contact the parish as soon as possible.

Registration forms can be found here.

The confirmation programme will commence on Sunday 30th January at 5pm. Candidates will then attend the 6.30 pm mass.

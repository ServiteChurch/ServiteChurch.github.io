2022 Confirmation Programme

For young people aged 14/15 years upwards who wish to be confirmed in 2023, registration is now open. Please collect a Confirmation Enrolment Form from the colonnade and return to the Parish office. A Baptismal Certificate must accompany the enrolment form unless baptized in this Church. Also, £20 towrds expenses. Class commencement date to be announced.

2023 Communion Programme

Parents of children in Year 3 or older wishing to make their First Holy Communion in the next academic year should collect an application form from the Colonnade. This must be submitted with a Baptismal Certificate (if not baptised in our Parish). To accompany these, a £20 contribution towards the cost of Holy Communion resources. Deadlines and commencement date of programme to be announced.

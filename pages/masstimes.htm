Chrismas mass ticketing system

Christmas masses will be by ticket only and these will be available from 11 December onwards from the church.

The tickets are colour coded.

The Light Blue is for single people sitting at the ends of the middle benches and marked with one green tick.

The Lilac are for couples from the same household in the middle of the long benches.

The Green are for family groups of 3 or more from the same household and these are in the side benches.

Please be guided by the stewards. Then we can seat more people safely and observe the government guidelines.

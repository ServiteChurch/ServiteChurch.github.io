Social Distancing guidelines for private prayer

The church is now open for private prayers every day between 10am and 12.30 pm.

Welcome back to our church.It is good to see you but for your own safety and for everyone else as well, please keep to these simple rules.

  1. Follow the one-way system
  2. Keep your distance 2 metres at all times
  3. Do not to touch anything, door-handles or statues
  4. Only sit in the places indicated
  5. Wipe your seat as you leave.
  6. Use hand sanitiser on the way in and on the way out
  7. Put all wipes in the bins provided
  8. The toilets are locked and not to be used
  9. We are asked to pray silently

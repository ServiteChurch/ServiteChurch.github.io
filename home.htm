Coronavirus: No Masses and Services until Further Notice

The church is currently open for private prayers every day between 10am and 12.30 pm. Strict social distancing will apply. Please see here for details.

The goverment has announced widespread easing of lockdown restrictions, effective from the 4th July and this easing applies to gatherings for Mass. While we are all keen to see the return of regular Masses as soon as possible, the logistical problems are involved are difficult. We are working hard behind the scenes, and are waiting on guidelines from the Diocese and so we cannot say exactly when, on or after this date, Masses will resume.

Please check our website for updates and if you have any queries please contact us.
